Burger Recipe

This is a Burger

A burger is made up of a piece of bread, vegatables, and beef

Mixing all these need a skill that we will learn some day as we expect chef in charge to train us soonest

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Loaf
  • Vegetables
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Boil the beef for 30 minutes
  2. Cut your vegetables in smaller chunks
  3. Fry your vegetables for 5 minuts
  4. Mix your beef and vegetables
